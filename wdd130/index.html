Know Our School

Every journey begins with a purpose. At our school, that purpose is to inspire young minds, celebrate individuality, and build a foundation rooted in compassion and curiosity. Discover where it all began—and why it matters.

Starting Year

In 2018, Daniel Herrera and his wife laid the foundation for what would become a place of joy. They began with a simple but powerful idea: to build a space where children feel safe, seen, and inspired every day.
Now, with over 7 years of experience the school has grown into a vibrant and respected educational community—proof that passion and vision can spark something truly enduring.

Our Mission

We strive to create an inclusive environment where children feel safe, valued, and inspired to explore who they are and what they’re capable of. Through meaningful relationships, joyful discovery, and purpose-driven learning, we guide each child on their unique path—supporting them as they grow into compassionate, confident individuals ready to impact the world.

Values

At the core of our school are values that shape not just how we teach—but how we connect, grow, and lead together.
-Respect – We honor every voice.
-Curiosity – We believe in learning through wonder and exploration.
-Joy – We learn through happiness.
-Growth – We progress every day.
-Community – We grow together.

Programs and Activities

We offer programs that spark curiosity and nurture every child’s interests. From music and arts to outdoor learning, bilingual development, and community celebrations, each activity inspires joyful learning and social-emotional growth.

Spring

As nature awakens, little explorers plant gardens, hunt for colors in blooming trails, and celebrate growth all around. Spring brings moments of wonder that only this season can offer. Children connect with the natural world as they learn about cycles of life, care for plants, and express new ideas through art and sensory discovery.

Apply by February 14

Summer

Long sunny days mean adventures by the shore, splashy games, and exciting local trips. Children explore freely, make new friends, and create sun-soaked memories that last. Whether it’s building sandcastles, visiting new places, or cooling off with water play, summer is full of energy, joy, and imagination.

Apply by June 15

Fall

As leaves turn gold, we wander through nature trails and crisp autumn air. Fall brings storytelling, hands-on crafts, and cozy moments that connect learning to the world around them. Children gather treasures from the forest floor, explore seasonal changes, and reflect deeply through quiet exploration and collaborative projects

Appy by July 30
